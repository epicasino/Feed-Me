About FeedMe

Discovering your perfect recipe has never been easier with our website. We take into account your dietary preferences, allergies, mealtime, desired prep-time, type of cuisine, and calorie requirements to curate a personalized selection of three recipes just for you. Whether you're vegan, keto-friendly, or have specific food restrictions, our quiz ensures that every recipe suggestion aligns with your unique needs. Say goodbye to endless searching and let us guide you towards delicious meals that fit your lifestyle and culinary desires.
